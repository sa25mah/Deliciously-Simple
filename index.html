Simple Omelette Recipe

A quick and easy dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette is made with beaten eggs, cooked to perfection, and optionally filled with cheese, vegetables, or meats.


Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, whisk eggs with salt and pepper. Add water or milk for fluffier texture if desired.
  2. Heat the pan: Use a non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Add butter or oil until hot.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour beaten eggs into the pan, tilting to spread evenly.
  4. Add fillings: Once edges are set and the center is slightly runny, add your preferred fillings.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette over the fillings and cook for one more minute. Slide onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve immediately. Add extra salt or pepper if needed.
